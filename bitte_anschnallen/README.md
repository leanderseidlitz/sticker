Bitte Anschnallen
========

Some devices may experience unforeseen acceleration. To ensure proper Arbeitssicherheit, it is therefore mandatory that you fasten your seatbelt!

#### Print details

The sticker has the format of `52mm * 72mm`.
It works best when printed even larger.
The included SVG file is an inkscape SVG, for your editing pleasure. You will need the font `Alte DIN 1451 Mittelschrift`.
